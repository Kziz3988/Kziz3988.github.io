First Strike Wiki

First Strike is a replica of Blindflug Studio'sFirst Strike: Classic. It has been developed in six months since December 2022. At first, it was just a 3D Earth simulator in a 2D coordinate system, used to preview mathematical analysis. However, as the developers in Blindflug Studio choosed to add several pay-to-win contents in the game, I decided to develop this replica, in order to pay a tribute to the game, as it brought abundant amount of happiness in my childhood.

The most difficult part of developing this game is not simulating 3D effects in the 2D coordinate system (just needs some vector and matrix calculation), or designing the strategy of in-game AIs, but optimizing rendering to allow it to run on various devices. Anyway, I managed to finish this project, and that's why you could read this wiki.
