Regimes

There are 9 different regimes in the game, each one corresponds to one of the 9 nuclear owners in reality. However, the concept design of the game do not represent any political stance on the real world, only indicates that the developer believes that such an fictional world would be interesting. Differing from First Strike: Classic, players do not need to unlock them by playing other countries or pay for them. Each regime has its own color, science level and initial regions. Apart from those, there is no difference between them.

Each regime has diverse real-time scores of threat to others, which is based on the number of controlled regions, the number of launching missiles and the number of finished researches (these are the same for every regime). In addition, the numbers of missile launched between every pair of regimes also participate in calculation. This makes players who choose powerful regimes like USA or EU are likely to suffer fierce attacks from other regimes in early game. But if they manage to survive and defeat several enemies, victory is within reach. On the other hand, if a player decides to use small regimes like PAKISTAN or EAF, he or she could easily expand in early game, but has to face some giant regimes later.

USA

USA seeks to maintain its position of the world's hegemony.
USA, also known as the UNITED STATES OF AMERICA, is the most advanced and poweful regime in the game. It is the only regime that starts a game with 5 finished researches. With proper operations, USA could control the entire 8 regions of NORTH AMERICA just 30 seconds after the game started. Moreover, the initial regions of USA have high research levels. Naturally, USA is the most susceptible regime to suffer from global strike.
The color of USA is from the blue part of its flag in reality.

RUSSIA

RUSSIA strives to strengthen its status in the NORTHERN HEMISPHERE.
RUSSIA, which spans the north of the supercontinent of EURASIA, is the biggest regime in the game. It has 5 large initial regions, making it hard to defeat all regions at once. RUSSIA usually remains whole after the first round of strike. For RUSSIA, expanding into NORTH AMERCIA and EASTERN ASIA is often more efficient than competing in WESTERN EUROPE or MIDDLE EAST.

EU

EU endeavors to hold primary clout in the NORTH ATLANTIC.
EU, shorter form of the EUROPEAN UNION, is the dominant force in WESTERN EUROPE. It also owns 5 initial regions, but these regions are quite small. When hostile regimes occupy nearby regions, they only need MRBMs to attack mutiple regions. This makes WESTERN EUROPE the most fiercely contested regions among various regimes. For EU, abandoning regions in WESTERN EUROPE and trying to expand to AFRICA in the early game might be a good strategy. When EU gets a foothold, it can wait for an opportunity to make a comeback.

UK

UK attempts to find new allies in NORTHERN EUROPE.
UK, abbreviation of the UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND IRELAND, is rather fragile in international strategy after Brexit and a new wave of colonial independence. It is surrounded by USA, RUSSIA and EU, with a very limited zone of influence. IRELAND, NORTHERN EUROPE and GREENLAND are the only remaining neutral regions. After the merge with IRELAND, further actions are on the cards.
The color of UK is from the green color extensively used in royal occasions in reality.

CHINA

CHINA desires to become a new pole of global order.
CHINA is a traditional regional power in EASTERN ASIA. However, surrounded by 4 nuclear owners, it is the regime with the most complex international environment in the game. Although CHINA is trying to exert its potency on CENTRAL ASIA and SOUTHEASTERN ASIA, due to its efforts to foster a good international image, its expansion pace is relatively slow. Expanding to the power vacuum zone in CENTRAL ASIA is a bright way to break the deadlock.

INDIA

INDIA aspires to become the overlord across the equator.
INDIA almost takes over the entire INDIAN SUBCONTIENT, except its old rival, PAKISTAN. After frustrated in the westward conquer, INDIA turns to fight against CHINA and EAF in SOUTHEASTERN ASIA. Since there are no competitors in the south, as long as INDIA takes the key intersections like MALAY ARCHIPELAGO, this regime closest to the equator can easily become a dominator spanning the SOUTHERN HEMISPHERE.

PAKISTAN

PAKISTAN schemes to be the leader of the ISLAMIC WORLD.
PAKISTAN, a small regime struggling to develop under the massive pressure of neighboring superpowers, just acquires nuclear weapons. However, it does not have enough capable missiles to carry nuclear bombs to target locations. PAKISTAN is the only regime having no initial researches in the game. What's more, it has only one initial region. These make PAKISTAN the most weak regime and the rockiest to win the game.

ASSYRIA

ASSYRIA pursues becoming a regional power in MIDDLE EAST and AFRICA.
ASSYRIA is a secularized region with religious freedom. Due to the increasingly brutal gaming of extraterritorial superpowers in MIDDLE EAST, ISRAEL and some Arab countries have finally put aside their greed and prejudices, seeking cooperation. Naming the nascent regime after the mighty empire once existed on this land is clearly a good choice.
